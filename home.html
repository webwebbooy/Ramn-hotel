Welcome to Raman Restaurant, where we bring the rich flavors of North India to your table. Located in the heart of Lal Ganj, we've been serving authentic cuisine with love and passion for over 15 years.

Our chefs use traditional recipes passed down through generations, combined with the finest ingredients to create dishes that warm your soul and delight your taste buds.
